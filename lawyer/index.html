Meet our Solicitors in London

Family law and divorce

Our family and divorce solicitors in London are known for their professional, proactive approach to securing resolutions for our clients. We can help with all areas of family law, child law and divorce services.

Family law covers a large number of different issues, and many of these can be both complicated and difficult on an emotional level. This is why you need an expert solicitor on your side, one that’s well versed in the very latest family law regulations, cases and developments. Slater and Gordon’s London family lawyers can advise on marriage, civil partnerships, cohabitation, separation and divorce, as well as parental rights and custody of children. We also have extensive experience in cases involving adoption, surrogacy, foster care, paternity, child support, domestic violence, parental rights and a number of other issues.

Whatever situation you’re experiencing, please call our sensitive, experienced family law solicitors here in London on 020 7657 1555 for assistance and expert advice. No matter how complicated, our lawyers will work tirelessly to achieve a resolution that suits your needs and benefits as many people as possible. Many of our lawyers are members of Resolution, an organisation which aims to reduce expensive litigation and resolve issues through mediation and negotiation, and our solicitors also practice collaborative law. This approach puts you, the client, and your needs at the heart of negotiations, solutions and settlements.
